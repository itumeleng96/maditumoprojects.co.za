About Us

Who We Are

Maditumo Projects emerged in response to the growing demand for innovative engineering and consulting services in South Africa, specifically in Limpopo. We are a 100% black-owned company, founded on the principles of creativity, expertise, and commitment.

With a team of experienced partners, associates, and consultants, we are driven by a vision to become a globally recognised leader in the industry, offering specialised solutions and fostering sustainable development.

Our Services

Civil Engineering Works
Kerbs, V-Drains, stone pitching, speed humps, road marking & signage, pothole patching, culverts, trenching, re-gravelling, manholes, and pipe laying for construction.

Electrical Engineering

Information Technology
